CX: Building Trust Through
Customer-Led Experience

Developing a customer-led experience to make a true connection with your audience is key to building trust and brand loyalty. At IMLS, we’ll bring together senior-level marketers to discuss how to use digital signals to uncover your customers’ intents and how to design an experience that not only meets their needs, but delights them at the same time.

What should I expect?

  • Hear best practices and lessons learned from industry experts who have recently led digital transformation projects
  • Learn about current and future trends from a leading customer experience expert at Forrester Research
  • Team up with other marketing leaders to delve into customers’ mindsets and develop creative solutions to user experience challenges
  • Uncover how personalization can help you build trust through relevancy

FAQ

Rise Interactive’s complimentary invite-only Internet Marketing Leadership Series is specifically designed to bring together senior marketing leaders and industry experts to workshop, discuss trends, network and leave with tangible and actionable items to apply within their organizations.

Why attend IMLS?

We know your time is valuable. Unlike many industry conferences, IMLS packs a wealth of insight into a local half-day event. There are no vendors, only marketers. It's your chance to build your network and learn from other leaders. Our programs are based around emerging industry topics and we use attendee feedback to optimize every event. Our goal is to make sure you’re getting the most out of your time with us.

What can you expect to get out of an IMLS event?

This is more than just a half day of lectures. You’ll hear a keynote presentation, but also have the opportunity to ask questions of industry experts and hear about practical application within their organizations, participate in interactive sessions, hear from a live consumer focus group, and have time to network with peers.

Who should attend IMLS?

The content and agenda for IMLS is tailored for CMOs, marketing vice presidents, and directors from enterprise-level brands, allowing for in depth peer-to-peer discussion.
